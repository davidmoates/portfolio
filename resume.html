Resume

I have here my work history and other skills. Something that is important to know, I never do just what my job title say. I always try to be more of a utility, and I try to always make myself available to help with any task.

Work History

I am currently working at a branch of Momentum Group, Eykon. We sell and distribute wallcovering and upholstery products. With the job title of Order Picker, my job mostly consist of shipping and receiving samples as well as product. We have to meet strict deadline. That being said Efficiency and Organization are the most important skills I have to get the job done.

I worked at 2 different sign shops where I was the Graphic Design and Installer. These jobs were the most stressful because of the deadline that were need to be met. I was able to work on Vehicle Wrap, Building Signage, Wall Wraps, Decals of all sizes, and warehouse signage. Efficiency and Organization were very importnat as well as keeping a clean work environment. I mostly work in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Gospel Broadcasting Network (GBN) is one of my favorite places I have worked. My offical title was Volunteer Coordinator, yet at GBN we wore many hats. I mostly head up getting volunteer to help with different tasks like mail outs, camera work, etc. I also did camera work, studio set building, setting up the cameras by running cables, etc. With this company you learn how to do everything. I also got experience working on live show. I worked a debate in front of a live audience. I still have a great relationship with GBN to this day. I had to use the full Adobe CC as well.

Volunteer Experience

At Southaven church of Christ, I help schedule, train, and run the equipment in the Audio/Video room. I also help teach different classes on Sundays and Wednesdays, and cut the grass from time to time.

We play sports, have devotions, and just fun for the whole week. The counselors are considered the children's parents for that time. It is always a fun and rewarding week.

My Brother-in-law (Kyle Taubken) is a filmmaker. He has graciously let me help him on his short films by running sound. So far I have worked on 4 short. I have linked my IMDB page to see those short.
